Project Details

My Role: UX Designer

Duration: 2 Days

Tools: XD, Photoshop, Illustrator

Teammates: Alexis Overell - UX Designer, Anthony Riojas, Michael Dudley, Reid Miller - Developers

Background

As part of the Hackathon, my team worked on creating a mobile application for homeless people. Our team had 2 days to come up with a workable deliverable that we could showcase to the judges.

Problem

With smart phone becoming more accessible thanks to programs like Obama Phone, homeless people need an effective means to locate human resources.

Solution

Creating an application for the homeless to find local resources will allow them to effectively acquire basic human needs such as food, clothing, shelther, and medication.

Research

Over half a million people experience homelessness in a single night according to a 2018 case study done by the Department of Housing and Urban Development (source). 62% of homeless youth own smart phones according to a case study be NCBI (source).
