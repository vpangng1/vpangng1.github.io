My Role: Visual Designer

Duration: 3 Weeks

Tools: Figma, Illustrator

Background

Quue is a mobile application that streamlines the entire restaurant checkout process using QR codes. Quue works by having the retail merchant generate a QR code from the Quue app. The customer then completes the transaction by scanning that code with their phone. As the visual designer, I was tasked with ideating on the designs and screens for the application.
