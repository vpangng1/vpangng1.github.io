About

Hi, my name is Victor and I'm a UX Designer who is interested in building products that are sustainable, intuitive, and impactful. I believe user-centered design should be the forefront of any product. My passion and interests lie within education, science, and technology to improve people's lives. Growing up I developed an affinity for the arts, but never thought I'd do anything useful with it. I eventually got into a career in information technology and graphic design where I learned to deliver exceptional support and strategic solutions to customers.

While learning about web development I became interested in how UX designers utilize design to conceptualize ideas for developers. That's when it occurred to me that many of the things we did were UX related, whether we realized it or not. An entire discipline that combined psychology and design to create meaningful experiences and products was something I decided was worth pursuing.

I'm an outdoorsman who enjoys hiking, recreational shooting, and woodworking. In my spare time I also like to read, exercise, and eat great food. I believe the experiences we accumulate throughout our life provides us with fresh perspectives that can lend itself to design.
