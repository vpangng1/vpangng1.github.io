ABOUT MY PERSONAL LOGO

My personal logo was created entirely in Adobe Illustrator. I went through a few different iterations of my personal logo before deciding on the finished design.

I started the project by drawing some preliminary sketches on a piece of notepad paper. I drew most of the designs using a fine tip pen. The idea behind the design was to do something with my first initial (V) and my last name (Ng). The "V" would form part of the "N" of my last name.

Using Illustrator and a scanned image, I translated my drawings into vector graphics. However, I felt that the first design was too simple and not creative enough, so I decided to redo the design. The finished product turned out to be something I really liked.
