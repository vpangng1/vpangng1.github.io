Photo & Video App

Building an intuitive app interface using digital design toolkits.

ABOUT PHOTO & VIDEO APP

The photo app interface was created using Sketch as part of a side project. Using the Craft plugin by Sketch, I was able to upload my prototypes into InVision, where I could play with some of the interactive elements and see how they would look on different mobile devices.

I created the interface and designs in Sketch. The idea behind the design was to create an experience that was intuitive and personable. I drew inspiration from the iPhone's wallpaper lock screen for the app's home screen.

I used a combination of free photos from Pexel Images and the data panel in Craft to fill in the images for the screen interface. I used vectors in Sketch to create the download and bookmark icons for the Photos screen.

I downloaded an iPhone Sketch mockup template from dribble to insert my designs into the screens. After completing my designs in Sketch, I used the Craft plugin to upload the prototypes into InVision, where I was able to experiment with how the screens would look like on different mobile devices and do further prototyping.
