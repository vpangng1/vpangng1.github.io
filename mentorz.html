Background

Mentorz is a mobile application that enables mentors and mentees to connect with each other based on values and expertise. As the Visual Designer, I was brought on to propose new design iterations and features for the mentorship experience.


The Mentorz app is still in development. Due to non-disclosure agreement there are certain aspects of the work I am limited to share. As a result, what I am showcasing below is not fully representative of the full product.

Swipe UI

The Mentorz app is based off of Tinder's UI, where the app recommends people who may be compatible with the user. Users can swipe left or right on each profile card to express interest in possible mentorship with the person. In addition to seeing recommended mentor/mentees, users can see trending mentors/mentees.

Profile Screen

Users can view individual profiles to learn about who they are, their occupation, rating, years of experience, social media profiles, expertise and values. In addition users can follow, message, and request mentorship with the person.

Match Tab

Users can can view their sent and received mentorship requests. Accepting the request or having your request accepted begins the mentorship process.
